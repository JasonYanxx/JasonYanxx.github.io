<div class="landing-intro">
  <p class="landing-intro__lede">
    I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Intelligent Positioning and Navigation Lab at
    <strong>The Hong Kong Polytechnic University</strong>. My work focuses on
    <strong>trusted autonomy</strong>, especially GNSS integrity monitoring, non-Gaussian error modeling,
    robust localization, and uncertainty quantification for safety-critical navigation systems.
  </p>
  <p>
    I received my Ph.D. in Aeronautical and Aviation Engineering from PolyU in 2025, after completing my
    bachelor's and master's training at Wuhan University. My recent research connects statistical reliability,
    learning-based navigation, and deployable estimation methods for real-world autonomous systems.
  </p>

## Research Focus {#research}

<div class="highlight-grid">
  <section class="highlight-card">
    <p class="highlight-card__eyebrow">Credible uncertainty quantification</p>
    <h3>Diagnosing uncertainty under noise and system model mismatch</h3>
    <p>
      I study when reported uncertainty can still be trusted under model mismatch, and how multiple diagnostics can be
      combined to separate noise-model issues from system-model misspecification.
    </p>
    <p>
      Representative papers:
      <a href="/publication/2026-03-01-IEEE-TIM-Credible-UQ">IEEE TIM</a>.
    </p>
  </section>

  <section class="highlight-card">
    <p class="highlight-card__eyebrow">Robust estimation</p>
    <h3>Heavy-tailed modeling and robust urban GNSS positioning</h3>
    <p>
      I develop robust estimators and heavy-tailed error models for urban GNSS, with a focus on maintaining positioning
      performance when nominal Gaussian assumptions do not hold.
    </p>
    <p>
      Representative papers:
      <a href="/publication/2026-03-17-LQLC-Urban-GNSS">arXiv</a>,
      <a href="/publication/2026-03-20-LAH-GNSS">arXiv</a>,
      <a href="/publication/2026-01-08-Cauchy-Overbound">NAVIGATION</a>.
    </p>
  </section>

  <section class="highlight-card">
    <p class="highlight-card__eyebrow">Integrity monitoring</p>
    <h3>Fault detection and integrity monitoring under non-Gaussian errors</h3>
    <p>
      I study theoretically grounded integrity monitoring for localization systems, including non-Gaussian overbounding,
      efficient fault detectors, and integrity monitoring for simultaneous faults.
    </p>
    <p>
      Representative papers:
      <a href="/publication/2024-08-26-PGO">IEEE TAES</a>,
      <a href="/publication/2024-09-16-GNSS-2024">ION GNSS+</a>,
      <a href="/publication/2024-10-21-MFDI">IEEE Sensors</a>,
      <a href="2024-10-18-FDE-GMM">IEEE TIV</a>,
      <a href="/publication/2026-01-15-Jackknife-ARAIM">Aerospace Systems</a>.
    </p>
  </section>

  <section class="highlight-card">
    <p class="highlight-card__eyebrow">Autonomous systems</p>
    <h3>Control-aided localization for vehicles and UAVs</h3>
    <p>
      I am interested in reliable localization pipelines that combine dynamics, sensing, and learning for operation in
      challenging environments where nominal models break down.
    </p>
    <p>
      Representative papers:
      <a href="/publication/2023-05-08-SI">IEEE TIV</a>,
      <a href="/publication/2023-04-24-PLANS-2023">IEEE/ION PLANS</a>,
      <a href="/publication/2023-01-23-ITM-2023">ION ITM</a>.
    </p>
  </section>
</div>
